## Research Papers

| Paper | Contribution |
|-------|-------------|
| [Chain-of-Thought Prompting (Wei et al., 2022)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2201.11903) | Introduced CoT prompting |
| [Tree of Thoughts (Yao et al., 2023)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.10601) | Branching reasoning framework |
